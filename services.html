Our Services

Comprehensive assisted living services tailored to your loved one's needs

At Sesay Healthcare Service, we provide dedicated and compassionate staff for in-home assisted living. Our trained caregivers deliver personalized support so your loved ones can maintain their independence, dignity, and quality of life in the comfort of their own home.

What We Offer

Our caregivers are trained to provide a wide range of supportive services

🛁

Personal Care Assistance

Support with bathing, dressing, grooming, and personal hygiene to help maintain comfort and dignity every day.

🚶

Mobility Assistance

Help with transfers, walking support, and fall prevention to ensure safety and confidence in movement around the home.

🍽️

Meal Preparation

Nutritious meal planning and preparation with dietary accommodations to meet individual health needs and preferences.

💬

Companionship & Social Support

Meaningful engagement, conversation, and activities to promote emotional well-being and reduce feelings of isolation.

🧹

Light Housekeeping

Laundry, tidying, and maintaining a clean environment so your loved one can live comfortably in a safe, organized home.
