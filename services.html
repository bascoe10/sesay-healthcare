Our Services

Support that brings comfort, dignity, and peace of mind — right at home.

At Sesay Healthcare Services LLC, we provide dependable in-home support that helps seniors and adults maintain independence, safety, and comfort in the place they love most. Our services are designed to meet everyday needs with compassion, respect, and cultural sensitivity. We proudly serve families across Philadelphia, Delaware County, Montgomery County, and Bucks County.

Personal Care

Helping with daily routines that support comfort, safety, and independence.

Our caregivers provide gentle, respectful assistance with Activities of Daily Living (ADLs), including:

  • Bathing & grooming
  • Dressing
  • Mobility & transfer assistance
  • Toileting & incontinence care
  • Meal assistance
  • Medication reminders
  • Personal hygiene support

This service is ideal for individuals who need hands-on help to stay safe and comfortable throughout the day.

Companion Care

Meaningful connection that brightens each day.

Loneliness and isolation can affect emotional and physical well-being. Our companion care services offer:

  • Conversation & social engagement
  • Light exercise & walks
  • Games, hobbies, and activities
  • Reading, puzzles, and mental stimulation
  • Safety supervision
  • Emotional support and companionship

We help clients stay engaged, active, and connected.

Homemaking & Household Support

Creating a clean, organized, and comfortable home environment.

Daily tasks can become overwhelming for many individuals. Our caregivers assist with:

  • Meal preparation
  • Light housekeeping
  • Laundry & linens
  • Grocery shopping
  • Errands
  • Organization & tidying
  • Trash removal
  • Dishwashing

We help maintain a safe, healthy, and welcoming home.

Respite Care for Families

Giving family caregivers time to rest, recharge, and breathe.

Caring for a loved one is meaningful — but it can also be exhausting. Our respite care services provide temporary support so families can:

  • Take a break
  • Attend work or appointments
  • Travel
  • Focus on personal needs

Your loved one remains in caring, capable hands while you take time for yourself.

Specialized Support

Tailored care for individuals with unique needs.

We offer additional support for clients who require more focused assistance, including:

  • Dementia & memory care support
  • Chronic condition support (non-clinical)
  • Post-hospital recovery assistance (non-medical)
  • Disability support
  • Fall-risk supervision
  • Safety monitoring
  • Daily routine structure & reminders

Our goal is to create a calm, supportive environment that promotes comfort and stability.

Transportation & Errand Support

Helping clients stay active and connected to their community.

We provide safe, reliable assistance with:

  • Doctor's appointments
  • Pharmacy pickups
  • Grocery shopping
  • Social outings
  • Community activities
  • Personal errands

Caregivers accompany clients to ensure safety and comfort every step of the way.

Customized Care Plans

Every family is unique — your care plan should be too.

We take time to understand your loved one's needs, preferences, routines, and personality. Care plans are flexible and can include:

  • A few hours a day
  • Full-day support
  • Weekday or weekend care
  • Short-term or long-term care

Your family's comfort and peace of mind come first.
