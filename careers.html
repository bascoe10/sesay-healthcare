Who We're Looking For

Experience is a plus, but we also welcome individuals who are new to caregiving and eager to learn.

  • Compassionate and patient
  • Reliable and punctual
  • Respectful and professional
  • A good communicator
  • Able to work independently
  • Dedicated to providing high-quality care

Caregiver Responsibilities

Your day-to-day role may include:

  • Assisting with bathing, grooming, and dressing
  • Helping with mobility and transfers
  • Providing companionship and conversation
  • Preparing meals
  • Light housekeeping and laundry
  • Running errands and grocery shopping
  • Accompanying clients to appointments
  • Offering safety supervision and emotional support

Requirements

  • Valid ID
  • Background check
  • Reliable transportation
  • Ability to lift, bend, and assist clients safely
  • Professional, respectful attitude
  • Authorization to work in the U.S.

Some requirements may vary based on state regulations.
