About Us

Who we are, why we serve, and what we stand for

Who We Are

Sesay Healthcare Services LLC is a trusted home care agency proudly serving Philadelphia, Delaware County, Montgomery County, and Bucks County. We provide compassionate, reliable in-home support for seniors, adults with disabilities, and families who need help maintaining safety, comfort, and independence at home.

Our caregivers assist with daily routines, companionship, and household needs — always delivered with dignity, respect, and cultural sensitivity.

Our Story

Sesay Healthcare Services LLC was founded in 2025 by Adama Sesay, Junior Sesay, and Rudolf Sesay. Though they share a last name, the three co-founders are not related by family. What unites them is something far more powerful: a shared vision to transform the quality of in-home care available to residents across the Greater Philadelphia Area.

Their journey began when all three recognized a common gap in the healthcare landscape. Too often, individuals requiring daily assistance were left with limited options that failed to address their full needs. Adama, Junior, and Rudolf set out to build something better — combining their skills and commitment to community well-being to create a service rooted in genuine compassion, professionalism, and a personal touch that larger organizations often lack.

From day one, the co-founders have been hands-on in every aspect of the business, from hiring and training caregivers to meeting with families and developing individualized care plans.

Why We Serve

Choosing care for a loved one is one of the most meaningful decisions a family can make. Sesay Healthcare Services LLC was created to make that decision easier — by offering support that feels personal, trustworthy, and rooted in genuine compassion.

Whether your loved one needs help with daily routines, companionship throughout the day, or support after a hospital stay (non-clinical), we are here to provide care that brings comfort and peace of mind.

Our Caregivers

Our caregivers are carefully selected for their professionalism, warmth, and dedication to serving others. Each caregiver receives ongoing training to ensure they provide safe, respectful, and high-quality care.

We match caregivers to clients based on personality, needs, and preferences — because the right match creates comfort, trust, and consistency.

Who We Serve

We proudly support individuals across Philadelphia, Delaware County, Montgomery County, and Bucks County.

  • Seniors aging at home
  • Adults with disabilities
  • Individuals recovering after hospitalization (non-clinical support)
  • Families needing respite care
  • Clients who need companionship, supervision, or help with daily routines
