Our Story

How three dedicated professionals came together to make a difference

Sesay Healthcare Service was founded in 2025 by Adama Sesay, Junior Sesay, and Rudolf Sesay. Though they share a last name, the three co-founders are not related by family. What unites them is something far more powerful: a shared vision to transform the quality of assisted living services available to residents across Southeastern Pennsylvania. Each brought years of experience, unique perspectives, and an unwavering belief that every individual deserves to receive care with dignity and respect in the comfort of their own home.

Their journey began when all three recognized a common gap in the healthcare landscape. Too often, individuals requiring daily assistance were left with limited options that failed to address the full spectrum of their needs. Adama, Junior, and Rudolf saw an opportunity to build something better, combining their complementary skills and deep commitment to community well-being. Together, they set out to create a service rooted in genuine compassion, professional excellence, and a personal touch that larger organizations often lack.

From day one, the co-founders have been hands-on in every aspect of the business, from hiring and training caregivers to meeting with families and developing individualized care plans. Their collective dedication has established Sesay Healthcare Service as a trusted provider that families throughout SE Pennsylvania can rely on for dependable, high-quality home care.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver compassionate, dignified home care that empowers individuals to live safely and independently in their own homes. We are dedicated to enhancing the quality of life for every client we serve by providing personalized assistance, fostering meaningful relationships, and upholding the highest standards of professionalism and respect. At Sesay Healthcare Service, we believe that exceptional care starts with treating every person as a valued member of our extended community.
